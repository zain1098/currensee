# CurrenSee Pro Home Screen Widget

## Features

The CurrenSee Pro home screen widget allows you to:
- View real-time currency exchange rates
- Convert amounts between different currencies
- Swap currencies with a single tap
- Change currency pairs by tapping on currency areas
- Refresh rates by tapping the amount area

## How to Use

### Adding the Widget
1. Long press on your home screen
2. Select "Widgets"
3. Find "CurrenSee Pro" in the widget list
4. Drag and drop it to your home screen

### Using the Widget

#### Amount Display (Top Area)
- **Tap to edit**: Tap the amount display area to open the widget editor in the app
- Shows the current amount being converted (default: 1.00)
- You can change the amount, from currency, and to currency in the editor

#### Currency Selection
- **From Currency (Left)**: Tap to cycle through 100+ currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, INR, PKR, CNY, AUD, CAD, and many more)
- **To Currency (Right)**: Tap to cycle through 100+ currencies (EUR, USD, GBP, JPY, INR, PKR, CNY, AUD, CAD, and many more)
- **Visual hint**: "Tap to change" text shows which areas are interactive

#### Swap Button (Middle Arrow)
- **Tap to swap**: Quickly swap the "From" and "To" currencies

#### Result Area (Blue Section)
- **Read-only**: Shows the converted amount and current exchange rate
- **No interaction**: This area does not open the app or perform any action

## Troubleshooting

### Widget Not Updating
1. Check your internet connection
2. Tap the amount area to open the widget editor and update settings
3. Remove and re-add the widget if needed

### Widget Shows "No Internet"
- Ensure you have an active internet connection
- The widget requires internet to fetch live exchange rates

### Widget Not Responding
- Try removing and re-adding the widget
- Restart your device if the issue persists

## Technical Details

- **Update Frequency**: Widget updates every 15 minutes automatically
- **API**: Uses exchangerate-api.com for real-time rates
- **Storage**: Settings are saved locally on your device
- **Permissions**: Requires internet access for live rates

## Notes

- The widget works independently of the main app
- All interactions are performed within the widget
- No need to open the main app for basic currency conversion
- Settings are synchronized with the main app when available
